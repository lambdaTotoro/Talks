\section{How To Ask}

\begin{frame}
\begin{center}
\huge
No, but \emph{seriously}!\\
How \emph{do} I ask people for their gender?!
\end{center}
\end{frame}

\begin{frame}
\frametitle{My best take:}
\large
Before asking:
\medskip

\begin{itemize}
\pause\item Make sure you \emph{need} the information.
\pause\item Make sure \emph{this} is the information you need.
\pause\item Make sure its \emph{mutable} in your model.
\end{itemize}
\bigskip\medskip

\pause\emph{And then ask!}\medskip

Tell them, what you need the answer for and give either none or as many options as is reasonable under the \uncover<5->{circumstances\footnote<5->{that's \emph{almost always} more than 2!}} but always also give a text field for free-form answers.


\end{frame}
